Company Background

Pitch Parfait Ice Cream Parlor is a local ice cream shop owned by Pitch Parfait Enterprises. We are dedicated to making tasty frozen treats. In our shop, customer satisfaction is a priority. We focus on good customer service and maintaining a safe and clean environment for all. Our mission is to bring happiness to the community with each scoop and to provide a place where families and friends can enjoy a sweet treat together. We believe in deliciousness, efficiency, cleanliness, and community involvement.

Pitch Parfait Enterprises headquartered in Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines is the owner of Pitch Parfait Ice Cream Parlor and all its branches nationwide. The company was established in 2017 by a group of ice cream connoisseurs: Sophia, George, Owen, Wendell, and Camilla.
