Frequently Asked Questions

The company's name comes from a memorable karaoke night with its 5 founders. The song, "Cups" from the hit movie "Pitch Perfect" sparked a brilliant idea for a business name and motto. "Pitch" represents the founders' love for music, while "Parfait," a layered dessert, reflects the layers of love and quality ingredients we put into our craft. Combined, "Pitch Parfait" means hitting the right notes with every scoop!

Pitch Parfait Ice Cream Parlor offers 4 kinds of products:
  • Parfait: Our bestseller! A treat with ice cream, flavored syrup, and fruit bits arranged in layers and topped with granola, sprinkles, or nuts.
  • Swirls: Soft-serve ice cream blended with mix-in ingredients like cookie pieces, brownie bits, or chocolate chips.
  • Milkshakes: Cold drinks made of ice cream, milk, and flavorings/syrup blended together until frothy.
  • Frappes: Cold drinks made of coffee, milk, sugar, water, ice and other selected flavorings blended together and topped with whipped cream.

This will largely depend on the cup size in which you order your treats in and what mix-ins and flavorings they might have! For more information, check out our Nutrition Facts page from the Menu.
